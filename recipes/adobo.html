Adobo

Description

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Black Pepper
  • Oil
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Rub salt unto the pork
  2. Pour oil into hot pan
  3. Saute Garlic and pork until golden brown
  4. Put water and vinegar into the sauteed pork
  5. Simmer for at least 45 min or until the fat renders
  6. Serve
